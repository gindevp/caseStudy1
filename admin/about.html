TRAINING REGULATIONS Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS Article 1. Scope of regulation and subjects of application 1. Regulations on training at CodeGym training institutions, including: training organization, assessment of learning results, obligations and interests of lecturers and students. 2. This Regulation applies to all officers\employees participating in training at CodeGym. 3. This Regulation applies to students studying at CodeGym. Each student is responsible for mastering and fully and strictly implementing the training regulations and regulations at CodeGym. Article 2. Definition and role 1. Courses The course is the training period at CodeGym from enrollment to completion. Each course consists of one or more modules designed to be closely integrated with each other, helping students develop and gradually perfect all the necessary knowledge and skills. Besides the main task of studying, students are also obliged to participate in other common activities of CodeGym such as collective activities, social work, etc. At the end of the course, students are granted a COURSE COMPLETE CERTIFICATION LEARN by CodeGym and some certificates of completion of online courses during the course of study. 2. Module A module is a relatively complete knowledge part of a course, designed to facilitate learners to acquire and gradually improve the entire knowledge of a training program in the learning process. Each module is taught by one or more instructors. All modules of the program are aimed at equipping students with knowledge and skills for lifelong learning and long-term career development. Each module includes working sessions such as theory hours, practical hours, series of activities: Seminar/Workshop/Career Consulting/Developer at work, etc.). The duration of each working session (slot) is 1.5 hours. . Digital learning system CodeGym uses 5 digital systems: CodeGymX, CodeGymSIS, CodeGymE, CodeGymP, CodeGymB, CodeGym Ada to improve learning efficiency, interaction, exchange of resources and information between departments, between trainers - coaches, instructors, tutors, mentors and students, and create a modern and effective learning environment when managing students' learning and learning process. Each CodeGym student, trainer, and employee is assigned a CodeGymID account to use CodeGym's 5 numbering systems. a) CodeGymX (james.codegym.vn): Is an online learning system (LMS - Learning Management System) for CodeGym students/officers/trainers. Students are assigned to the modules of the study program. Students will learn videos, reading passages, slides and quizzes before starting the theory session; view and submit exercises and assignments; task completion; Receive certificates of completion for online courses. Instructors can view the module materials; grading exercises and assignments for students; and assess student performance. b) CodeGymSIS (andy.codegym.vn): Is a management information system used in education (CodeGym SIS - CodeGym Student Information System). CodeGymSIS is used to centrally manage student data for all CodeGym centers. CodeGymSIS acts as a common user database management system for all CodeGym systems. c) CodeGymE (ada.codegym.vn): Is a CodeGym ePortfolios competency profile system, where CodeGym students "display" their skill sets with demonstrations of competencies (skills/knowledge) consciousness) that. Each student account on CodeGymX will be linked to a set of competency profiles on this management system. Students and lecturers can manage and update records\evidence for each capacity defined by the system. Employers\investors have the right to view the published profiles on the system to evaluate and make appropriate recruitment decisions. d) CodeGymB (bob.codegym.vn): A system that helps programmers learn and practice algorithms and problem-solving skills. The exercises in CodeGymB are designed to lead and improve developer skills at each level. e) CodeGymP (ken.codegym.vn): Is a work management system for students at CodeGym based on Kanban board. Each student will have a backlog of the work to be done during the learning process. This is the most intuitive way to track student work progress on a daily basis. f, CodeGym Ada (ada.codegym.vn): A system built for the purpose of managing digital competency profiles for CodeGym students. Thereby serving the connection needs between businesses and students of CodeGym. 4. Online communication/exchange channel: Slack CodeGym uses Slack as a channel for information exchange between students and students and with trainers about knowledge, skills, activities, technical issues, learning situation, discipline, and experience exchange. Each class will have its own channel. Students will be added to the #general channel to receive quick information from the Training Director, Instructor and trainers, interact with students from other classes or former students. In addition, direct can be used to directly message 1 or more people. 5. Roles a) Training director Person in charge of public affairs TRAINING REGULATIONS Chapter 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS Article 1.
